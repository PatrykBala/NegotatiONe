Information Negotatione

Why to Buy

Stores your stuff in iconic style while helping protect global forests and support furniture makers.

The Mid-Century 6-Drawer Dresser is made from sustainably-sourced solid wood (certified to FSC® standards) in a Fair Trade Certified™ facility, meaning that each one purchased protects the world’s forests and directly benefits the artisan who crafted it. Retro details—like beveled edges, cross-braced legs and streamlined hardware—turn its clean-lined silhouette into an instant classic. Its warm Acorn finish is water-based, so no harsh chemical smell when it’s unpacked.

Details + Dimensions

Overview
  • 60"w x 18"d x 36"h.
  • Made from sustainably sourced wood certified to FSC® standards. Learn more.
  • Solid eucalyptus wood with Acacia-veneered drawers in a water-based Acorn finish.
  • Beveled front edges.
  • Antique bronze hardware.
  • Six drawers open on solid wood glides.
  • Made in a Fair Trade Certified™ facility. Learn more.
  • Anti-tip kit hardware (included) is highly recommended to provide protection against tipping of furniture.
  • Imported.

Dimensions
  • Overall product dimensions: 60"w x 18"d x 36"h.
  • Top drawer dimensions (2): 25.6"w x 14.6"d x 5.6"h.
  • Bottom drawer dimensions (4): 25.6"w x 14.6"d x 6.6"h.

Packaging
  • Number of boxes: 1.
  • Shipping package: 60.5"w x 22.5"d x 37"h.
  • Package weight: 230 lbs.

Assembly Instruction

Assembled upon arrival via In-Home Delivery. Anti-tip kit hardware (included) is highly recommended to provide protection against tipping of furniture. View assembly instructions.

Care

Lift when moving to avoid damage to base or legs. Wipe clean with a soft, dry cloth. To protect finish, avoid the use of chemicals or household cleaners. Hardware may loosen over time. Periodically check to make sure all connections are tight.

Shipping + Returns

Unlimited flat rate delivery – furniture + oversized items

Our Flat Rate Delivery fees are based on where you live, not how much you buy. With Unlimited Flat Rate Delivery, an unlimited number of eligible furniture and select oversized items can be delivered for a single flat rate.

All of our Flat Rate items include our premium White Glove Service—at no extra charge—for oversized furniture and select fragile items. With White Glove, your order is brought into your home by a skilled delivery team on a pre-scheduled date, unpacked in the room of your choice and fully assembled, but not installed*. All packaging materials are removed upon completion.

Easy Returns

To help with holiday gift giving, we are extending our return policy for stocked items purchased between 10/30/2017–12/24/2017. If you or a gift recipient are dissatisfied with a gift purchased during this time, present a gift or original receipt by 1/31/2018 for a full refund of the merchandise value. Quick Ship upholstery items must be returned within 7 days of the order receipt date. Outside these timeframes, we will replace or refund your purchase against manufacturer’s defects ONLY.

An original receipt or gift receipt is required. Prior purchases are not eligible for price adjustments.

How to Return
  • Return to Stores: You can return most non-furniture items to your local west elm store for free.
  • UPS: For items under 70 lbs, you can print a return label from our Returns Policy page and bring to a UPS location.
  • For non-Special Order furniture and other oversized items, please contact our customer service department at 1.888.922.4119.
Items not eligible for return:
  • Special Order (SPO) items, including custom rugs and furniture
  • Drop Ship items, with the exception of wall art
  • Monogrammed items
  • Mattresses
  • Gift cards
  • Final sale items (with prices ending in $.X7 or $.X9)
  • Items damaged through normal wear and tear
Complete Details

For full details, please visit our Returns Policy page. For further assistance, please call our customer service department at 1.888.922.4119 or send an email to customerservice@westelm.com.
